VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Indian national cricket team. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India 1. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport 1. Kohli has contributed to India’s successes, captaining the team from 2014 to 2022, and winning the 2011 World Cup and the 2013 Champions trophy 1. He is among only four Indian cricketers who have played over 500 matches for India 1. Kohli is the fourth-highest run-scorer in international cricket and stands second in the list of cricketers by number of international centuries scored

Early life and education

  • Kohli joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy at the age of nine and trained under his first coach, Rajkumar Sharma
  • He started his youth career with the Delhi Under-15 team
  • He attended Vishal Bharti Public School and later moved to Savior Convent School
  • His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, was a homemaker

National recognition

  • During the 2003–04 season, Kohli was appointed as the captain of the under-15 team.
  • Virat Kohli led the Indian Under-19 cricket team to victory in the 2008 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup
  • In the final against South Africa, Kohli scored 19 runs and took a catch to help India win by 12 runs
  • He was awarded the Man of the Tournament award for his performance in the tournament

International success

  • He has played 111 Test matches and scored 8,676 runs with an average of 49.3
  • He has also played 286 One Day Internationals (ODIs) and scored 13,437 runs with an average of 59.1
  • Kohli is the fourth-highest run-scorer in international cricket and stands second in the list of cricketers by number of international centuries scored
  • In the ongoing ODI World Cup 2023, Virat kohli is close to breaking Tendulkar's ODI hundreds tally

Personal life

  • Kohli's romantic association with Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, which commenced in 2013
  • On 11 December 2017, the couple exchanged nuptials in an intimate ceremony in Italy.
  • On 11 January 2021, the couple had their first child, a daughter, who was named Vamika.
  • In 2018, Kohli disclosed that he had made the decision to adopt a vegetarian diet
